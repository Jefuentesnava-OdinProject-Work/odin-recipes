Air Fried Frozen Salmon

Description

From your freezer to dinner plates, these flaky, moist, and tender air-fried salmon fillets are ready in just about 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1.4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 (6 ounce) frozen salmon fillets

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 390 degrees F (190 degrees C). Spray the air fryer basket with cooking spray
  2. Mix Dijon, honey, balsamic vinegar,garlic, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl.
  3. Place frozen salmon fillets in the prepared aur fryer basket. Place in the preheated air fryer and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Brush Dijon mixture over the fillets and continue to air-fry until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 5 more minutes.
